# Players and Games
- Pl -- # of Players: Number of Players used in Games
- Age -- Average Age: Age is weighted by minutes played
- Poss -- Possession: Possession calculated as the percentage of passes attempted
- MP -- Matches Played: Matches Played by the player or squad
- Starts -- Game or games started by player
- Min -- Minutes
- 90s -- 90s Played: Minutes played divided by 90

## Performance
- Gls -- Goals: Goals scored or allowed
- Ast -- Assists: Assists
- G+A -- Goals + Assists: Goals and Assists
- G-PK -- Non-Penalty Goals: Non-Penalty Goals
- PK -- Penalty Kicks Made: Penalty Kicks Made
- PKatt -- Penalty Kicks Attempted: Penalty Kicks Attempted
- CrdY -- Yellow Cards: Yellow Cards
- CrdR -- Red Cards: Red Cards

## Expected Goals and Assists
- xG -- xG: Expected Goals: Expected Goals. xG totals include penalty kicks, but do not include penalty shootouts (unless otherwise noted).
- npxG -- npxG: Non-Penalty xG: Non-Penalty Expected Goals
- xAG -- xAG: Exp. Assisted Goals: Expected Assisted Goals, xG which follows a pass that assists a shot
- npxG+xAG -- npxG + xAG: Non-Penalty Expected Goals plus Assisted Goals

## Progression
- PrgC -- Progressive Carries: Carries that move the ball towards the opponent's goal line at least 10 yards from its furthest point in the last six passes, or any carry into the penalty area. Excludes carries which end in the defending 50% of the pitch
- PrgP -- Progressive Passes: Completed passes that move the ball towards the opponent's goal line at least 10 yards from its furthest point in the last six passes, or any completed pass into the penalty area. Excludes passes from the defending 40% of the pitch

## Per 90 Minutes
- Gls -- Goals/90: Goals Scored per 90 minutes
- Ast -- Assists/90: Assists per 90 minutes
- G+A -- Goals + Assists/90: Goals and Assists per 90 minutes
- G-PK -- Non-Penalty Goals/90: Goals minus Penalty Kicks made per 90 minutes
- G+A-PK -- Non-Penalty Goals + Assists/90: Goals plus Assists minus Penalty Kicks made per 90 minutes
- xG -- xG/90: Expected Goals per 90 minutes
- xAG -- xAG/90: Expected Assisted Goals per 90 minutes
- xG+xAG -- xG + xAG/90: Expected Goals plus Assisted Goals per 90 minutes
- npxG -- npxG/90: Non-Penalty Expected Goals per 90 minutes
- npxG+xAG -- npxG + xAG: Non-Penalty Expected Goals plus Assisted Goals per 90 minutes
